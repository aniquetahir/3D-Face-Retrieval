Multidimensional scaling with vector extrapolation v 1.1
http://tosca.cs.technion.ac.il
(c) Michael Bronstein, 2008

DESCRIPTION:

Least-squares multidimensional scaling (MDS) implemented using SMACOF algorithm with 
reduced rank extrapolation (RRE) and multigrid (MG) acceleration.
